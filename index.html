⚠️ QR CODE SECURITY RISKS

Security experts warn that restaurant QR codes are prime targets for fraud:

  • Easily replaced: Scammers print fake QR codes and stick them over real ones
  • "Quishing" attacks: Malicious codes redirect to phishing sites that steal credit card info
  • No visual verification: You can't tell where a QR code goes before scanning
  • Payment theft: Fake ordering platforms capture your financial data
⚠️ The FBI and FTC have issued warnings about QR code scams. Every scan is a risk.
